Our History

Rapidis Rafting was founded in 2017. The Nile River has been part of Ugandan life for a very long time. Many people have lived, worked, and enjoyed its waters through history. At Rapidis Rafting, we invite you to join thousands of others who have shared in this amazing experience. When you come rafting with us, you become part of a community connected by the river's energy and beauty.
